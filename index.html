About Our Club

Welcome to the Phuket Cruising Yacht Club (PCYC), established seven years ago by Captain and founder Brent McInnes. As part of our master plan, we aim to inform and entertain you, making PCYC your ultimate home base in Phuket. You are always welcome here, where guests pay the same low prices as our members!

We've continually evolved to offer a comprehensive range of services, now including brokerage & charter services and even a law office.

The PCYC is the island's full-service yacht club, providing a welcoming atmosphere for sailors and enthusiasts alike.

Your Stay & Amenities

Enjoy a hassle-free experience with our motorcycle, car, bungalow, RIB, tender outboard engines, and dinghy rentals. Your dinghy will be safe on our flood-lit dinghy lines in front of the clubhouse. Our showers are open to all, and you'll find plenty of outlets for charging devices. Our accredited AYS sailing school is actively training in sailing and powerboat seamanship.

Our Services

Port & Specialized Services

As an authorized port-entrance agent and Royal Thai Customs yacht visa extension service, the PCYC simplifies your check-in/check-out with Immigration and handles customs accreditation and extensions for your boat. Most boats receive a six-month stay on arrival, which can be extended for another six months before requiring departure.

We are proud to be the island's full-service yacht club, offering:

  • Thai Customs Visa Extensions
  • Brokerage
  • Charter
  • Salvage
  • Law Office
  • Second Hand Boat Parts For Sale
  • Boat Minding
  • Rental of Car, Bikes, RIBs, Beach Bungalows
  • Access to preferred contractors

Captain Brent is always just a phone call away for assistance.

Club Life & Dining

Our newly renovated clubhouse, complete with new beach decks, is open from 8 am until late. Enjoy happy hour from 5-7 pm with reduced prices on alcohol. Our full bar features the PCYC's signature Margarita made from scratch, Bloody Marys, and a wide selection of local and imported beers. Here at PCYC, you can enjoy ice-cold beer, great food, and meet & chat with world cruisers & salty expats! Our well-traveled bar manager, Yai, possesses a wealth of local information.

Brent offers a delicious menu of Thai and Western-style food, specializing in Tex-Mex favorites like quesadillas, nachos, and tacos. Start your day with Eggs Benedict and fresh ground coffee, or enjoy our world-class fish and chips & hamburgers. All our food is MSG-free. Complimentary Phuket Tide Tables, local marine guides, and magazines are also available.

Taco Tuesday at PCYC Chalong!

Everyone's favorite night is back! Taco Tuesday at the Phuket Cruising Yacht Club Chalong is in full swing. Enjoy cheap Tex-Mex & pizza, 50 baht Gin and Rum, and 80 baht frozen Margaritas. Join us for music, drinks, beer, and a T-shirt lotto, every Tuesday on the beach at Chalong next to Arch 39 hotel. See you around sunset till late!

Music & Events

The PCYC boasts a great PA system, mixing board, and mic stands, encouraging yachties to showcase their musical talents. We often feature local entertainers like the talented Gene Neilson.

Our clubhouse is available free of charge for members and guests as a party venue, with catering options or the flexibility to bring your own food while we supply the bar. We can accommodate up to 100 people and hold a 2 am closing license.

Stay tuned for our upcoming twilight race series! PCYC members are also welcome to join the Phuket Yacht Club (PYC) race series. The best way to stay informed is to request to join our Facebook group.

Membership & Benefits

Both members and non-members pay the same low prices, fostering a welcoming atmosphere for our large expat following and over 100 diverse members, from oil tycoons to sea gypsies. Khun Thitti, a retired Bangkok lawyer and co-founder, is often at the clubhouse and available for sound advice.

We also offer extended-period boat minding ("basic care") for Bt2,000 a month, including daily checks of mooring lines, waterline, and companionway, endorsed by many insurance companies.

The new storm-water culverts in Chalong have improved our beach, creating sandy areas for beaching catamarans and powerboats for maintenance. Our resident black poodle, Teddy, heads up reception, alongside three friendly soi dogs and a large boat cat, Meow Yai.
