About Us

The Phuket Cruising Yacht Club (PCYC) was founded with a passion for sailing and a vision to create a vibrant community for yachting enthusiasts in one of the world's most beautiful cruising grounds. Located on the stunning island of Phuket, Thailand, our club offers unparalleled access to the Andaman Sea, with its crystal-clear waters, secluded islands, and breathtaking natural beauty.

Our mission is to foster a safe, enjoyable, and sustainable yachting experience for all members. Whether you are a seasoned sailor or new to the world of yachting, PCYC provides a welcoming environment, state-of-the-art facilities, and a diverse range of activities.

Our Heritage

Established in 1995, PCYC has grown to become a cornerstone of the yachting community in Southeast Asia. Our rich history is built on a tradition of seamanship, camaraderie, and a deep respect for the marine environment.

Facilities

We boast a modern marina with full-service berths, a well-equipped maintenance yard, a clubhouse with dining and lounge areas, and a comprehensive marine supply store. Everything you need for your yachting journey is at your fingertips.

Community & Events

PCYC is more than just a marina; it's a thriving community. We organize regular regattas, social events, cruising rallies, and educational workshops, providing ample opportunities for members to connect, learn, and explore together.

Join us at the Phuket Cruising Yacht Club and embark on your next unforgettable maritime adventure.
